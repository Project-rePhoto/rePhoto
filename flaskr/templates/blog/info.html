What is rePhoto?

rePhoto allows you to take pictures that aligns exactly with a previous photo.

rePhoto makes repeat photography easier, so you (or a group!) can align and take many pictures of a place or an object. These pictures are a visual record of change -- pictures of trees or streams or beaches over time are useful to measure the effects of erosion and climate change; pictures of home improvement projects (or even yourself) can serve as a visual timeline of events.

What is the purpose of rePhoto?

The core utility of Project rePhoto stems from its identity as a citizen science project which allows for a comprehensive visual timeline of change for each project’s subject, granting researchers more time to spend on analytical work while citizens not directly involved with the research can provide samples as they come across a project’s subject.

Solution idea

Contributors uses the web application to take repeat images of the subject. User line up their camera with a half see-through overlay of the previous image. These picture provide visual timeline of changes. Anyone with access to the website should be able to contribute to the project.
